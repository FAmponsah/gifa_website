OUR VISION

Our Vision is to break multicultural barriers through arts and culture.


OUR MISSION

The mission of GIFA is to strengthen the relationships between Irish Nationals and Ghanaians by reaching out to the locals to promote the full integration of Ghanaians into the Irish society for their socio-economic development.

 

GUIDING VALUES

    Respect for different cultures.
    Respect for the right of the individual.
    Serving our communities.
